Fixes LoKr training with Krea 2 FP8 base models and two display issues on the regularization page

### Fixed

- **LoKr training now works with Krea 2 FP8 base models (#454)**
  LoKr runs using an official FP8 base model previously stopped on the first step because an FP8 operation was unsupported. Existing training configurations work as-is after upgrading; datasets do not need to be rebuilt.

- **The regularization source selector has its visual states back (#454)**
  The borders, indicators, and selected state for “AI prior / Booru fetch” disappeared in v0.20.0. They are restored, and both labels stay on one line.

- **Long natural-language tags no longer overflow the regularization list (#454)**
  Long sentences in “Exclude train top tags” previously wrapped across multiple lines and covered following content. They now stay at a single-line height with an ellipsis, while the full text remains available on hover.
