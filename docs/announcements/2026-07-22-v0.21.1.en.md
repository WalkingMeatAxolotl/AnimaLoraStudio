Fixes incorrect training results when block swap is enabled

### Fixed

- **Training results are no longer affected when block swap is enabled (#466)**
  In v0.21.0, setting “Block swap” above 0 for training produced incorrect gradients. Nothing failed or warned during the run, but the resulting LoRA was of lower quality. With Prodigy-family optimizers (PPSF) it showed up as the learning-rate estimate jumping sharply and the run breaking down; with fixed learning-rate optimizers such as AdamW there was no visible symptom. **If you trained a LoRA in v0.21.0 with block swap enabled, retraining after upgrading is recommended.** Runs that left it at the default of 0 are unaffected, as is block swap during test image generation.
